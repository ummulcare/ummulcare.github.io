What I do or use at some of JFK's Terminals:

  • Terminal 1: the main terminal that I use for Air France, Royal Air Maroc, Saudi Airlines, and ocassionally Turkish airlines.
  • Terminal 4: secondary terminal that I use for Delta and Emirates.
  • Terminal 5: Although not currenty in use, I used to take the B15 bus into JFK to transfer to the air train when I lived 10 minutes away from the airport.
  • Terminal 7: for Kuwait Airways, Ethiopian Airlines, and Air Canada.
